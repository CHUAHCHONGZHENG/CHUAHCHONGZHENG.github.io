Wonderlands x Showtime

Musical / Show

Wonderlands x Showtime is an eccentric musical show troupe dedicated to restoring the glory of the Phoenix Wonderland stage. Led by a self-proclaimed star and a genius inventor, they combine high-energy performances with wild, inventive gadgets. Every show by Wonderlands x Showtime is a chaotic journey designed to make the entire world "Wonderhoy!"
