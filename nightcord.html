25-ji, Nightcord de.

DARK POP / ELECTRO

25-ji, Nightcord de. is a mysterious underground music circle that operates solely through the Nightcord voice chat app at 1:00 AM. Each member struggles with their own inner darkness and seeks a sense of belonging within the digital void. By creating hauntingly beautiful music, 25-ji, Nightcord de. attempts to find salvation for themselves and their listeners.

Band Member Biographies

The reclusive composer of 25-ji, Nightcord de., Kanade Yoisaki spends every hour at her keyboard, desperate to write a song that can save others.

The "empty" lyricist of 25-ji, Nightcord de., Mafuyu Asahina hides behind a perfect mask while searching for her lost self in the silence of the night.

The passionate illustrator of 25-ji, Nightcord de., Ena Shinonome battles her insecurities and a hunger for recognition through her digital art.

The enigmatic video maker of 25-ji, Nightcord de., Mizuki Akiyama uses fashion and playfulness to hide a heart that fears the judgment of the world.
