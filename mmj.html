IDOL POP

MORE MORE JUMP! is an unlikely idol unit consisting of one persistent dreamer and three girls who had previously retired from the industry. They aim to break the traditional idol mold by performing with a DIY spirit and boundless positivity. Through their efforts, MORE MORE JUMP! hopes to deliver hope and smiles to fans who have lost their way.

Band Member Biographies

The fiery spirit of MORE MORE JUMP!, Airi Momoi uses her veteran idol experience and "tsundere" charm to push the group to professional heights.

The ethereal beauty of MORE MORE JUMP!, Shizuku Hinomori balances her elegant stage presence with a hilariously clumsy and directionless daily life.
